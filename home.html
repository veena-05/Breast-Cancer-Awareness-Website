About Breast Cancer Awareness Bill, 2022

  • The Breast Cancer Awareness Act, 2022, introduced by the Indian government, aims to enhance breast cancer screening, prevention, and education across the country.
  • This act provides free mammograms and cancer screenings in government hospitals for women aged 40 and above, emphasizing early detection and timely intervention.
  • Key clauses of the act include:
    • Mandatory awareness programs in rural and urban areas.
    • Establishment of dedicated breast cancer units in select regions.
    • Financial assistance for treatment in underprivileged communities.
  • The provisions are currently available in major states including Maharashtra, Delhi, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, and West Bengal, with plans for further expansion in the coming years.
